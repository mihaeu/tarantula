Gear

28.12.2013

By Michael Haeuslmann

 

Update:This sections requires some major rework, a lot of things have been changed, especially since I'm not travelling in cold Europe anymore and because I'm no longer cycling by myself.

When I check out travel blogs I always go straight for the gear section so I will try to be as precise as possible in describing what I took with me.

Gear, of course, is always subject to change as you go along and stuff breaks, is no longer needed or needs to be changed due to climate changes etc.

This is what I took with me when I left home:

Bicycle

Frame Santos Travelmaster 26" Aluminium
Lowrider #1 Tubus Ergo
Lowrider #2 Pletscher Athlete 2B
Rear Rack Tubus Cargo
Saddle Brooks B67
Shifters Shimano LX Rapidfire
Deraillers Shimano XT
Cassette Shimano LX 11/32
Chain Shimano HG93
Wheels Shimano XT Hub with Rigida Sputnik CSS rims
Tyres Schwalbe Marathon 50mm
Pedals Shimano PD-A530
Chain Guard SKS Chainboard
Brakes Shimano XT V-Brake

Camping

Tent Wechsel Pathfinder Travel Line
Hammock Hennessy Hammock
Matress Thermarest Z-Lite
Pillow Thermarest Compressible Pillow (M)

Kitchen

Stove Primus Omnifuel + 1l Fuel Bottle
Cookware Esbit CW2500HA
Mug Sea to Summit X Mug
Bowl Tupperware FlatOut!
Cutlery Victorinox Knife and Fork + Noname Spoon
